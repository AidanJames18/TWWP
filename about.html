Tiger Woods

Born to Be the Best.

Tiger Woods is a professional golfer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest golfers of all time. He was born on December 30, 1975, in Cypress, California, and began playing golf at a very young age. His father, Earl Woods, was a retired U.S. Army lieutenant colonel and his mother, Kultida, is from Thailand. Woods was exposed to golf at a very young age and started playing at just two years old. By the age of three, he was already hitting balls on the golf course and showing great promise as a golfer.

Woods was a prodigious young talent and quickly began to make a name for himself in the golf world. He won his first junior tournament at the age of six and went on to win numerous other junior events throughout his childhood and teenage years. Woods attended Stanford University on a golf scholarship and continued to excel on the course at the collegiate level, winning several NCAA championships and other awards.

Woods turned professional in 1996 and quickly established himself as a dominant force in the sport, winning numerous tournaments and major championships. He is known for his incredible skill on the golf course, as well as his fierce competitiveness and mental toughness.
